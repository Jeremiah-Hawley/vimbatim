Vimbatim — First Launch Instructions
=====================================

This is a closed beta build. It isn't signed by Apple or Microsoft yet, so
your OS will show an "unknown developer" warning the first time you open
it. This is expected — here's how to get past it.

macOS
-----
1. Unzip/unmount and drag Vimbatim.app to Applications (or run it from
   wherever you put it).
2. Right-click (or Control-click) Vimbatim.app and choose "Open".
3. A warning dialog appears. Click "Open" again. (Just double-clicking the
   app will refuse to launch and won't offer this option — you must
   right-click the first time.)
4. If macOS still refuses to open it, open Terminal and run:
     xattr -cr /Applications/Vimbatim.app
   then try opening it again.

Windows
-------
1. Unzip the folder anywhere you like.
2. Double-click vimbatim.exe.
3. Windows SmartScreen will show a blue "Windows protected your PC" box.
   Click "More info", then click "Run anyway".

After that first launch, the app opens normally like anything else.

Found a bug or crash?
----------------------
If the app crashes, a log is written automatically:
  macOS/Linux: ~/.vimbatim/crash.log
  Windows:     %APPDATA%\vimbatim\crash.log

Please share what you were doing plus that crash.log file (if the app
crashed) in the feedback channel — the exact build you're on is shown in
Settings, at the bottom of the panel.
